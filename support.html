Frequently asked questions

I'm a restaurant — how do I join Nosh?
Tap "Join the waitlist" on noshtheapp.com and select "I'm a restaurant." We'll text you when we're ready to onboard you. Nosh is currently launching in Bushwick, Brooklyn.
I want to drive for Nosh — what do I do?
Sign up at noshtheapp.com under "I'm a driver." Once approved, you'll complete a background check through Checkr and receive instructions to download the app.
How much does Nosh cost for restaurants and drivers?
$0 until you've earned $500 through Nosh. Once you hit the $500 threshold, a $50/month subscription fee is deducted from your subsequent earnings. DoorDash and Uber Eats take 15-30% of every order — forever.
What does "earned $500 through Nosh" mean?
It means the cumulative gross earnings (before platform fees) you've received through Nosh orders since joining. Once your lifetime Nosh earnings cross $500, your subscription activates on your next payout cycle.
After my subscription activates, how does the monthly fee work?
Each month, the first $50 of your earnings pays the platform fee. After $50 is covered for that month, 100% of your remaining earnings are paid out to you. The balance resets on the 1st of each month.
Is there a delivery fee for customers?
Customers pay the menu price plus tax and an optional tip. A small platform service fee may apply in the future; we'll always be transparent about any fees at checkout before the order is placed.
How do I cancel an order?
Tap "Cancel" on your active order screen. Before the restaurant begins preparation, you'll get a full refund. Once preparation starts, partial refunds may apply. Orders can't be cancelled after pickup by the driver.
How do I delete my account?
Email hello@noshtheapp.com with the subject "Delete my account" and we'll handle it within 30 days.
I had a problem with an order — what do I do?
Use the "Report a problem" button in the app on the order screen. You can also email hello@noshtheapp.com with your order number and details.
When is Nosh launching in my neighborhood?
We're starting in Bushwick, Brooklyn. Sign up for the waitlist and we'll let you know as we expand.
How does Nosh keep users safe?
All drivers complete a background check through Checkr before accepting deliveries. Customers and drivers rate each other after every order. An emergency button is available in the app during active deliveries, and every report is reviewed and documented.

Accessibility

We're committed to making Nosh accessible to everyone. If you encounter accessibility issues, please email hello@noshtheapp.com and we'll address them promptly.
